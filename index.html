A Brief Overview

The Wingo Lab is a genetics laboratory at the Atlanta Veterans Administration and Emory Unversity's Center for Neurodegenerative Disease led by Aliza and Thomas Wingo.

The primary focus of the lab is to understand the genetic basis of Alzheimer's Disease.

We gratefully acknowledge our funders: the Veterans Administration, the National Institutes of Health, Emory University, the To Remember Foundation, American Psychiatric Association, and the Brain and Behavior Foundation (formerly NARSAD).
