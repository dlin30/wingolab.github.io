A Brief Overview

The Wingo Lab is a genetics laboratory at the Atlanta Veterans Administration and Emory Unversity's Center for Neurodegenerative Disease led by Aliza Wingo, M.D., M.Sc. and Thomas Wingo, M.D..

The primary focus of the lab is to understand the genetic basis of Alzheimer's Disease, psychological well-being, and psychological resilience.

Our work has been featured in the Huffington Post, the Emory Health Magazine, and CNN.

We gratefully acknowledge our funders: Veterans Administration, National Institutes of Health, Emory University, To Remember Foundation, American Psychiatric Association, and the Brain and Behavior Foundation (formerly NARSAD).
